Overview

MKTableCellIAP provides an interface for In App Purchases. The cell has a a label and a purchase button. This cell works with MKIAPController to make purchases via the inApp Purchase API.

If you are using this cell directly you need to provide a MKIAPObserver and follow its protocol.
