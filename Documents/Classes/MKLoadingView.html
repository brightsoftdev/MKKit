Overview

MKLoadingView Provides a small view that informs the user of a process taking place. There are two types of loading views that can be displayed.

  • MKLoadingViewTypeIndicator: Shows a UIActivityIndicatorView.
  • MKLoadingViewTypeProgressBar: Shows a custom drawn progress bar.
