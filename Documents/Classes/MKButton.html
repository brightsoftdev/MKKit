Overview

MKButton provides specalty buttons for various use. There are currently three types of buttons:

  • MKButtonTypeDarkBlue: a dark blue button similar to UIBarButtons
  • MKButtonTypeGreen: a brighter green button
  • MKButtonTypeIAP: a InApp Purchase button, mimics the purchase buttons from the appStore

Note MKButton objects will look for resources in the MKControl-Resources bundle. Ensure this bundle is added to your project for proper function.
