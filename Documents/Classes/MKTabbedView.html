Inherits from MKView : UIView
Declared in MKTabbedView.h

Overview

MKTabbedView provides the view for the MKTabbedViewController. Tabs and content display is controlled by this class.

MKTabbedView controller implements the MKTabbedViewDataSource, and MKTabbedViewDelegate.
