Overview

MKTabbedViewController implents an interface for display views usings tabs. Three tabs are displayed at the top of the view, each displaying a unique view.

Content of the views are provided by the MKTabbedViewDataSource, and changes can be monitored with the MKTabbedViewDelegae. MKTabbedViewController conforms to both of these protocols.
