Overview

MKIAPViewController provides a store front interface for your inApp Purchases. This is drop in store front, just call the default initalizer and place the instance in your view controller stack.

Results of purchase and request are sent to the MKIAPObserver. The MKIAPObserver has one required method.

This class works along with MKIAPController and MKTableCellIAP.
